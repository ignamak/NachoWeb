Making games is something personal, it's the perfect combination
of art and technology, it's a cultural product and it has to be done responsibly.
Making them while having fun is my passion.

Hello, I'm Nacho, a passionate technical game designer dedicated to infusing joy into people's lives through the magic of videogames. Fascinated by the human concept of "fun", its meaning and why it is important for us. I also like to explore its potential societal impact. Currently focused on continuous growth, I simultaneously delve into the realms of game design and programming.

With a Bachelor's degree in videogame development and a Master's in game design, my journey spans the indie scene, small studio projects, and AAA experiences. Proficient in Unreal Engine 4 & 5 and Unity/C#, my key strengths lie in soft skills—effective teamwork, leadership, and project management.

Beyond the gaming world, I maintain an active lifestyle, engaging in sports and gym activities. I'm initiating a podcast book club and have a deep love for boardgames. Since winning a Chinese NES clone in a raffle, my lifelong passion for gaming has evolved from consoles to PC, with a penchant for social play, action, strategy, RPGs, and a decade-long affinity for indie games and roguelikes.

Let's connect and explore the endless possibilities at the intersection of technology, creativity, and fun!
